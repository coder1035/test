A note from the heart
My dearest Aafi,

Three months ago, something extraordinary began. I still find myself amazed that I get to call you mine. You came into my life not so long ago, yet somehow it feels like you have always been a part of it, like a chapter I did not know was missing until I started reading it.

You are unlike anyone I have ever known. Not just because of your mind, the scholar in you who quietly cleared entrance after entrance and then turns around and says "I am not that smart", but because of the way you pour yourself into everything you do. The card you made for me, the birthday present you so carefully put together, the bari decor, the plant that you gave me, the keychain, the dishes that you made for me, and not to forget the beetroot halwa I never imagined I would finish but somehow did and kept asking for more. These are not small things, Aafi. They are you, and you are extraordinary.

And then there is the artist in you. You sketch, you paint, you create entire worlds from a blank page. You even drew my favourite cricketer for me, and I still cannot fully believe you did that. You love cars with a passion most people reserve for people. You live on the spicy side of life and make no apologies for it. You carry this creative fire in everything you touch, and I find myself lucky every single day to witness it up close.

I think about the way you carry every outfit like you chose it for a reason, the way you measure out a room before moving a single thing, the way you spotted the perfect kurta for me before I had even begun to look, the way you just say "ye pehno" and suddenly we twin. You notice the world more carefully than most people do, and somehow that makes me feel more seen too.

We have had two beautiful trips, one to Alibaug and one international to Malaysia. Both were awesome experiences, and both were very unique. For me, it was my first ever international trip, and having you next to me made it even more special.

We have also had some fights and some differences of opinion, but that is natural, and I hope it only makes our relationship stronger. Bhel bhi khatti aur meethi ho tabhi achhi lagti hai.

So here we are, three months in, and I am still that person who smiles every time I see you. I hope I never stop. Happy anniversary, Aafi. To three months, and to every season yet to come.

With all my love,
Usama 🤍
